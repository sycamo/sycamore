S H O R T    F O C U S   

Williams Handmade, London


Sarah Williams established her own accessories label in 2010 after studying at The London College of Fashion, and her exquisite work is already winning her much acclaim from the fashion press. With an emphasis on maintaining traditional craftsmanship, her beautiful leather goods are all made by hand using prime British bridle leather and solid brass fittings. Shop her designs exclusively on NJAL.
B L A C K   S H E E P   D U   J O U R

Pola Thomson, New York


Chilean designer Pola Thompson is based in New York, where she is currently working on her own womenswear and accessorise line. She has a sound background in design, having studied at both London’s Central Saint Martins and New York’s Parsons The New School of Design. Her most recent collection, #7 is a vision of effortless, intelligent shapes, clean lines and accent colours. A selection of her pieces is now exclusively available on NJAL's online shop!
T H E   N J A L   D I A R Y   S U G G E S T S

Central European Fashion Days, Budapest

From Friday 7th to Sunday 9th June, Hungary’s capital will be playing host to this year’s Central European Fashion Days. The initiative aims to provide a platform for the most talented designers from the region of Central Europe, and NJAL's Mary and Marika will be there to scout talents and report back.
